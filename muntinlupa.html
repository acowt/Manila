Exciting Place to Visit
in Muntinlupa City







    1. Jamboree Lake

 

  
This is the smallest natural lake in the country, a favorite hang-out spot for nature lovers, as
well as ordinary passers-by looking for a cool respite from the tropical heat. The lake has been 
in existence since time immemorial. 



    2. Alabang Town Center

 

  
owned by Ayala Malls and is frequented by those from the southern part of Manila such as those 
from Las Piñas and Parañaque. The mall features several high-end international boutique chains 
such as Gap and Marks & Spencer. There’s a Rustan’s Department Store in the mall as well.




    3. Bellevue Manila

 

  
Rest easy at The Bellevue Manila! You’ll find all you need here at Bellevue Hotel in Alabang
situated right at the heart of its bustling business center.

Choose from the 456 rooms and suites available for the best stay possible. The rooms were 
carefully designed with your comfort in mind, and each room features soft beds and spacious and 
stylish interiors for a good night’s sleep.

Go on a gastronomic adventure with fantastic dining options, and work on your wellness with the 
hotel’s state-of-the-art facilities such as the spa, gym, and swimming pool. With excellent 
service, spacious and comfortable rooms, and top-notch facilities and amenities, this five-star 
hotel in Alabang promises a delightful experience for its valued guests.
